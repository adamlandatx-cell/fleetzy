Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting with Fleetzy

How long does the approval process take?

Most applications are approved within 24 hours. We run a quick background check and verify your documents. Once approved, you can pick up your vehicle the same day.

What do I need to apply?

You'll need a valid driver's license, proof of income (bank statements or rideshare earnings), and a selfie for identity verification. The entire application takes about 5 minutes.

When do I pay my weekly rental fee?

Weekly payments are due every 7 days from your pickup date. We'll send you automated reminders 2 days before payment is due. You can pay via Zelle, CashApp, Venmo, PayPal, or card.

Is the $500 deposit refundable?

Yes! The $500 security deposit is fully refundable at the end of your rental, provided there's no damage to the vehicle and all payments are up to date.

Are your vehicles approved for Uber and Lyft?

Absolutely! All Fleetzy vehicles meet the requirements for Uber, Lyft, and DoorDash in Houston. You can start driving immediately after pickup.

What happens if the car breaks down?

We provide 24/7 roadside assistance. If there's a mechanical issue, we'll either repair it quickly or swap you into another vehicle at no additional cost.

Can I rent for less than a week?

Our minimum rental period is 2 weeks, but you can continue renting week-to-week after that for as long as you need.
