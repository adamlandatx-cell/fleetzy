Everything you need to know about renting with Fleetzy

You'll need a valid driver's license, proof of income (bank statements or rideshare earnings), and a selfie for identity verification. The entire application takes about 5 minutes.

Weekly payments are due every 7 days from your pickup date. We'll send you automated reminders 2 days before payment is due. You can pay via Zelle, CashApp, Venmo, PayPal, or card.
